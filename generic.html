School Closures caused by COVID 19

Here is a case study about the effect of COVID-19 to schools.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, schools worldwide faced significant disruptions and challenges. To prevent the spread of the virus, many countries implemented widespread school closures. This meant that students had to stay home, and traditional in-person classes were suspended. School closures varied in duration, ranging from a few weeks to several months, depending on the severity of the pandemic in each region.
